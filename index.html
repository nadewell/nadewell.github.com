Keep track or your earnings and your tradings. Trading Book shows you how much you've earn in how many tradings on dashboard. Trading Book stores all data in an offline database so you can access them all time.

Use Trading Book to:

  • Store and manage trading records.
  • Store and manage trading rules that you're going follow for profitable trade.
  • To see your total earning and total trades.
